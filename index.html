About Me

My projects tend to sit somewhere between physics and engineering, giving me the chance to move between theory, simulation, CAD, fabrication, and experimental testing. I enjoy using the physics behind a system to guide real design decisions, then seeing how those decisions hold up once the system is built.

Space exploration is what first pulled me toward engineering and physics, and it remains the direction connecting the work documented here. The work I find most interesting combines demanding physics with real hardware and a meaningful engineering problem.
